About Me

I am a passionate AI researcher specializing in federated learning, zero-shot learning, multimodal large language models (LLMs), and generative AI. With a Ph.D. in Software Convergence from Sejong University, I have developed advanced solutions in anomaly detection, video summarization, and intelligent multimedia systems. My work integrates cutting-edge AI techniques to address real-world challenges in surveillance, energy informatics, and activity recognition. Currently a Postdoctoral Researcher, I actively collaborate on global projects, pushing the boundaries of AI innovation through high-impact research and publications in leading journals. My goal is to advance adaptive, scalable, and transformative AI technologies for societal and industrial benefit.
